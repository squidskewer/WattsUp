About WattsUp

Our Mission

WattsUp is committed to aiding the accessibility of energy for all individuals regardless of household income and location. Our main priority to achieve this goal is to educate and empower individuals to make mindful decisions about their energy usage so that less fortunate people may have more allocation to these resources.

Who We Are

Created by passionate high schoolers in the PNW area aiming to improve the future of the world that many generations will come to face.

Our Impact

Through our resources, we help individuals reduce energy consumption and carbon footprints, fostering a more environmentally conscious world. Join us in making a difference.
